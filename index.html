About Us
Lakshmi Apparels was Incepted in the year 1996, We have been in the garment manufacturing field with 25 years hands full of experience, specialised in creating high fashion, delicate kids wear. Besides kids wear, the company also manufactures all kind of men’s & ladies garments using the latest print techniques and updated fashion trends. Our company is located in the southern part of India ,Tirupur. The Company takes care of their client’s with total dedication and commitment with a team of young and aspiring professionals.

Our Company is having a vertical setup as well as experienced professionals in all levels of operation to meet buyer requirements in time. We have a production capacity of 0.15 million garments per month. As Clothing Manufacturer, we believe that clothes should be made to last. We use high quality materials and construction techniques to ensure that our clothes can withstand years of wear and tear. Our clothes are designed to be comfortable and stylish, so you can look great and feel confident in your clothing. We offer a wide range of sizes and styles to suit every body type, and our clothes are available in a variety of colors and patterns. Whether you're looking for casual wear or formal attire, We has the perfect outfit for you.
