About Us
We are one of the leading knitted garments manufacturers cum exporters in Tirupur. Founder Mr. R.Bharath Kumar join himself with this very big industries since 1994 and through various export banner. After lot of analysis, he gathered full knowledge, techniques about the knitted garments manufacturing and exporting, he started his own firm in the name of M/s SYSTEM APPARELS (P) LTD since 2001 and our new business firm name of M/s RBK APPARELS is situated in Tirupur.

We have constantly grown and upgraded our internal production facilities. We have also increased control over the complete process involved in the manufacture of knitted & woven garments. The fact that we are dealing with our customers for a long time and that most of our business (over 70% to 80%) is repeat business- speaks for our performance. We are looking for developing a business relationship with a long term perspective and hope to satisfy your sourcing requirements by not only supplying quality garments on time that meet international standards as far as shrinkage, colour fastness and other parameters are concerned, but we will also provide you world class service.
