About Us
We are one of the leading knitted garments manufacturers cum exporters in Tirupur. Founder Mr. R.Bharath Kumar join himself with this very big industries since 1994 and through various export banner. After lot of analysis, he gathered full knowledge, techniques about the knitted garments manufacturing and exporting, he started his own firm in the name of M/s SYSTEM APPARELS (P) LTD since 2001 and our new business firm name of M/s RBK APPARELS is situated in Tirupur.
